About Us

EmphasisO E-SUMMIT is a two day event orgnized by Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship. The event aims at the core value of building a startup and solving real life problems thereby.
It is the first of its kind initiative taken in JMI to help nurture the spark of entrepreneurship in students. It will also lead to innovative ways of job creation in the university.

CENTRE FOR INNOVATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, established in the year 2014 provides a unique platform to the students of the university to implement their innovative ideas.
The Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE) at JMI has two divisions namely: The Innovation Centre and Livelihood Businness Incubator. The Centre is dedicated to serve the nation and the mankind through their initiatives and hopes to be helpfull in the sustainable development of the Nation.

OBJECTIVES

*To connect and grow more new business.
*Access Indian markets and business.
*To promote and launch innovation driven products and services.
*To establish fruitful partnerships and collaborations with governments, industry and institutions.

FAQ's

Is EmphasisO free?

It’s not. Prior to the event registration will begin on the website. There will be a minimal amount to be charged for spectators’ assurance.

How & When to register?

Prior to the event registration will begin. It will be notified on the website itself. Also, on all the social media pages of EmphasisO.

Who can apply for Startup Internship Fair?

Prior to the event all can apply for the internship fair, then candidates will be shortlisted as per their capabilities and respective experiences. Then, they could face startup interviews.

What is FinFO?

FinFO is a news aggregator app, a startup in Jamia. EmphasisO is an initiative by FinFO founders.
