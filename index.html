About Us

EmphasisO E-SUMMIT is a two day event orgnized by Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship. The event revolves around the core value of building a startup and solving problems thereby.
It is the first initiative taken in Jamia to enlighten ehe spark of entrepreneurship in students; this will also lead to creative way of job creation in the university.

CENTRE FOR INNOVATION AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship Jamia Millia Islamia, Ne Delhi, established in the year 2014, provides a unique platform for the students of university to fulfill theie innovative ideas
The Centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE) at JMI has two divisions namely: the Innovation Centre and Livelihood Businness Incubator. The Centre is dedicated to serve nation and the mankind through these initiatives and hope it will be helpfull in the sustainable development of the Nation.

FAQ's

Is EmphasisO free?

It’s not. Prior to the event registration will begin on the website. There will be a minimal amount to be charged for spectators’ assurance.

How & When to register?

Prior to the event registration will begin. It will be notified on the website itself. Also, on all the social media pages of EmphasisO.

Who can apply for Startup Internship Fair?

Prior to the event all can apply for the internship fair, then candidates will be shortlisted as per their capabilities and respective experiences. Then, they could face startup interviews.

What is FinFO?

FinFO is a news aggregator app, a startup in Jamia. EmphasisO is an initiative by FinFO founders.
